# 02 — Positioning

## Positioning statement

> **For** premium Shopify Basic/Grow brands selling wholesale,
> **Stockly** is the B2B wholesale app
> **that** makes their wholesale portal look as polished as their consumer storefront,
> **unlike** BSS, Bold, or B2B Hub which prioritize features over brand experience.

---

## One-liner candidates

1. ✅ "The wholesale app for brands that care about how their B2B portal looks." (primary)
2. "Premium B2B wholesale on Shopify Basic — no Plus required."
3. "Wholesale, beautifully done."
4. "Your brand. Your wholesale portal. Without Shopify Plus."

---

## Brand pillars

### 1. Premium aesthetic first
Everything we ship looks beautiful by default. Even the empty states. Even the error messages. Especially the error messages.

### 2. Less is more
Fewer features, pulidas. Not the "kitchen sink" approach BSS takes. Each feature does one thing and does it brilliantly.

### 3. Mobile-first
Designed for the buyer who orders from their phone between meetings. Tables, forms, cart — all work flawlessly on a 375px viewport.

### 4. Theme-native
Looks like part of the merchant's theme, not a bolted-on app. Inherits colors, typography, spacing from theme settings whenever possible.

### 5. Fast
Modern stack (Remix + edge functions). Storefront blocks load in <500ms. Admin loads in <2s.

---

## Anti-positioning (what we are NOT)

- ❌ NOT for stores with 1000+ SKUs and complex catalog logic (they should use BSS or Plus)
- ❌ NOT the cheapest option (we're premium-priced, $39-149/mo, not $9-29)
- ❌ NOT a full B2B platform replacement (we extend Shopify, don't replace it)
- ❌ NOT for stores that don't care about brand aesthetic (default Shopify is fine for them)

---

## Customer profile (Ideal Customer)

**The "Diptyque-style" SMB DTC brand:**
- 50-500 SKUs
- $100k-$2M annual revenue
- 5-50 wholesale leads per month
- Cares deeply about visual identity
- Uses a premium theme (Ella, Impulse, Prestige, Horizon)
- Has a designer or design-conscious founder
- Wholesale is 10-30% of revenue today, wants to grow it to 40-60%

**They look like:**
- Premium jewelry brands (like Piro)
- Indie beauty (skincare, fragrance)
- Boutique fashion / accessories
- Artisan home decor
- Specialty food & beverage
